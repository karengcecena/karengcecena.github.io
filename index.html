ABOUT

I'm a software engineer who is passionate about using technology to drive social impact and promote diversity in tech. With a background in healthcare, I find joy in communicating with and helping others, and I'm excited to use my skills to make a difference on a broader scale.

During my final quarter at the University of California, San Diego, I discovered the command line in a Microbiology Lab, which ignited my interest in programming.

Since then, I've been actively seeking opportunities to expand my technical skills. I confirmed my love for programming in an Intro to Python course and went on to attend Hackbrights Software Engineering Bootcamp. I have experience with languages like Python and JavaScript, frameworks like Flask and ReactJS, and industry tools like Git.

As a child, when I wasn’t outside running around and adventuring in the desert with my friends, you would find me snapping pictures and videos, solving sudokus, reading, and re-watching movies & tv shows (we all know they don’t make rom-coms like they did in the late 90s/early 2000s).

Now, you can still find me enjoying those same activities, except instead of getting my daily fix of problem-solving through sudokus, I get it through coding!
